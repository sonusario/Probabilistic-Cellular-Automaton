Probabilistic Cellular Automaton

Separate survival and birth probabilities by neighbor count. Conway's Game of Life is the default: live cells survive at 2 or 3 neighbors, and dead cells are born at 3 neighbors.

Survival probabilities

Used when a cell is currently alive. The value is the chance it stays alive next generation.

Birth probabilities

Used when a cell is currently dead. The value is the chance it becomes alive next generation.
